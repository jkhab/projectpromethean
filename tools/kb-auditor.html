Knowledge Base Auditor

Before you ground an AI on your help center, you need to know what state it is in, because a confident answer built on a stale article is worse than no answer. Paste a Zendesk help-center URL and this reads every published article and flags what is quietly rotting: pages years out of date, near-duplicates, and articles too thin to actually answer the question. It reads the public help-center API directly from your browser.

Works on any public Zendesk help center.
