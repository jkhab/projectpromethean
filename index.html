About

An operator who builds.

I spent ten years inside enterprise B2B SaaS running support, success, and implementation: the migrations, the workflows, the escalations, and the systems underneath all of it. In the past year I started building production AI systems myself and shipping them: a paid iOS app, document-extraction pipelines, and an internal support assistant.

That combination is the practice. AI does the implementation. Operations judgment decides what gets built, what will break, and what it should cost. Everything I recommend to a client is something I have already run in production on my own products.

It starts with a conversation about your workday. Tell me what you wish you didn't have to do, or what takes so much time it's costing you growth. There's probably a system for it, and I'll tell you honestly whether it's worth building.
