About

Built for small businesses, not enterprise budgets.

The ability to automate your work has never been more accessible. The tools are there — most small business owners just haven't had the time to figure them out, or don't know where to start. That's where I come in.

We start with a conversation about your workday. Tell me what you wish you didn't have to do, or what takes so much time you'd rather be spending it somewhere else. There's probably a solution — and I'm here to help figure out what that looks like for you.

I've spent 10+ years inside fast-moving SaaS companies building exactly these kinds of systems. Small businesses deserve the same edge. That's what this is.
